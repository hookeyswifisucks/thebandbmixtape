Big Sean - Bounce Back



×

“Last night took an L, but tonight I bounce back, Boy, I been broke as [heck], cashed a check and bounced back.”

Now this song may now perfectly represent their relationship, but Big Sean’s concept of ‘bouncing back’ fits it well. In the lines above, Big Sean shows that no matter what happens, he is able to ‘bounce back’. This happens to Benedick and Beatrice when they break up, but realize that they need each other and remarry.
