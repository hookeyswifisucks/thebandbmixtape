X

“Last night took an L, but tonight I bounce back, Boy, I been broke as [heck], cashed a check and bounced back.”

Now this song may now perfectly represent their relationship, but Big Sean’s concept of ‘bouncing back’ fits it well. In the lines above, Big Sean shows that no matter what happens, he is able to ‘bounce back’. This happens to Benedick and Beatrice when they break up, but realize that they need each other and remarry.

X

“No one wants to die alone, They don't wanna miss the show”

In the lines above, Lil Uzi Vert explains that nobody wants to end up alone in life and it could be inferred that he thinks that everybody needs someone else in life. These lines do a very good job of representing how Benedick thought earlier in the story that he wanted to remain a bachelor and that he didn’t need or want to marry, but eventually realized that he did want to marry, after realizing that Beatrice loved him.

X

“Yes I'd do anything (Anything?), Anything for you, I’d do a bid, lose a rib, bust a cap, trusting that, Run up to heaven doors, exchange my life for yours.”

This song represents how Benedick was willing to do anything for Beatrice’s love, even kill Claudio. When Beatrice asked him to, he didn’t take much persuasion to decide that he was willing to do it. Jay-Z’s and Benedick’s loyalty to there significant others seem very similar when examining the line above.

X

“I don't really care if you cry, On the real you should've never lied, Should've saw the way she looked me in my eyes, She said, ‘Baby, I am not afraid to die.’”

This song represents Benedick and Beatrice’s love/hate relationship. Lil Uzi Vert and his ex-girlfriend Brittany Byrd had an on and off relationship over the course of the past few years, ending in later 2017 near the release of the song. This is similar to how Benedick and Beatrice split up and got back together when they realized that they loved each other.
